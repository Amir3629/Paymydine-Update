# Executive Summary for Founders (Phase 9)

## What the platform definitely has
- A multi-tenant restaurant architecture with tenant-aware DB switching.
- A broad restaurant operations admin: orders, menus, categories, reservations, tables, kitchen display, staff/roles, notifications, POS, and payment configuration.
- Guest-facing ordering flows with menu/table routes and mobile-friendly frontend structure.
- Payment orchestration code paths for multiple providers and webhook handling.
- Germany-focused compliance-related technical artifacts (Fiskaly) and biometric GDPR-oriented services.

## What the platform probably has (needs controlled validation)
- Broad wallet/payment method support depending on provider and merchant eligibility.
- Meaningful analytics dashboards beyond baseline widgets.
- End-to-end gift card operations in production-grade UX.

## What is impressive / differentiating
- Tenant provisioning plus DB clone model for rapid multi-brand rollout.
- Unified operational surface combining FOH/BOH features with payment/webhook architecture.
- Presence of both fiscalization and biometric data-protection modules in the same platform scope.

## What needs validation before aggressive selling
- Tenant-by-tenant live payment method availability.
- Legal readiness package per market (starting Germany).
- Production hardening level (given many historical backup files in repo).

## Recommended asset creation order
1. Proof-first product screenshots pack (guest flow + admin flow + KDS + payments).
2. One-pager (safe claims only).
3. Sales deck v1 (no unverified metrics).
4. Technical brochure for IT/compliance buyers.
5. Germany compliance sheet with legal disclaimer.
6. Persona-specific objection-handling scripts.
